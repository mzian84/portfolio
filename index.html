About Me

I’m Muhammad Zian, a final-year Computer Science and Engineering student at Visvesvaraya Technological University, passionate about building innovative software solutions and exploring emerging technologies. With a strong foundation in data structures, algorithms, and programming (Python, C, HTML, CSS), I enjoy turning ideas into functional, real-world applications.

I’m driven by curiosity, collaboration, and the ambition to contribute to fields like software engineering, AI, and IoT, while continually sharpening my skills through real-world challenges.
